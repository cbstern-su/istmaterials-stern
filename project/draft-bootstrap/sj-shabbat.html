On Friday Nights...

In light of recent circumstances in the COVID-19 climate on campus, Syracuse Jews is now bringing the festive Shabbat meals to you. Our Shabbat-To-Go meal packs are stocked full with everything you need to make your Friday night a special celebration. Be sure to place an order and stop by before sundown every Friday for some delicious food to enjoy in the safety of your own home!

Shabbat Must-Haves

These items will be available to you in every single Shabbat-To-Go pack to ensure you have everything you need to observe the holiday in good spirits. It's not a complete Shabbat without some of these favorites:

  • Candles
  • Wine or Grape Juice
  • Challah Bread (plain, everything, maple, and sweet crumb flavored)
  • Prayer Book
  • Good Company
