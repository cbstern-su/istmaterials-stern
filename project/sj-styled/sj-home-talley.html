Syracuse Jews — Our Mission

Syracuse Jews is a nonprofit organization that aims to be the warmest home for Jewish students at Syracuse University. Since its establishment in November 2019, Syracuse Jews has hosted countless holiday cobservations, festive meals, prayer services, social events, and classes. Welcome to the place where you can channel your most meaningful Jewish experiences for an even more menaingful college experience!
